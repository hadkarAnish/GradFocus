Mission statement

Mission objective

Our mission is to guide students to determine which school best suits their requirements. Our website provides a list of universities for three streams – MBA, MSIS and MSBA. It has the information of all the universities listed, based on rank. The interested customer’s profile is stored on the website once he’s registered. The customer can access the university information and based on his credentials, the customer can compare universities and their ranks which are suitable for his profile. The GradFocus consultants will provide the customer with the list of most appropriate colleges based on their overall profile.
